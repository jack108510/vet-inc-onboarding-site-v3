What We Implement
Features We Build Into Your PMS
These aren't generic recommendations — we configure and deploy each of these directly inside your practice management system.
99 Cent Price Rounding
We update your entire price list to .99 endings — a proven strategy that increases perceived value and can meaningfully improve revenue without raising prices. Done in one pass, directly in your PMS.
Automated Receiving
We configure your PMS to automatically update inventory quantities when shipments arrive. No manual receiving, no data entry delays — your stock levels are accurate in real time.
Smart Reorder & Inventory Management
We set minimum stock thresholds for every critical item in your inventory. When levels drop, reorder suggestions are automatically generated — so you never run out of medications or supplies unexpectedly.
Low Stock Alerts
Real-time notifications sent to the right people the moment something drops below threshold — before it becomes a problem for your patients or your workflow.
Inflation-Adjusted Pricing
Using our Price Analyzer report as a starting point, we identify every stale service and implement updated pricing directly in your PMS — not just flagging it, but actually making the changes.
Ongoing Support & Optimization
We don't disappear after setup. As your clinic grows or your PMS updates, we stay available to adjust configurations, add new automations, and keep everything running smoothly.
Advisory Services
Expert Guidance, Not Just Software
Beyond implementation, we provide the strategic insight to help clinic owners and managers make better decisions. Think of us as an operations consultant who also knows your PMS inside out.
Pricing Strategy Review
We analyze your full service price list against CPI benchmarks and competitor context, then provide clear recommendations on where to adjust — and by how much.
Workflow Audit
